About Wingman

We designed Wingman with the intent to bring more safety to traveling on foot, particularly on college campuses. One of the first tips for safe travel on foot is to never walk alone, if possible. Wingman is here to act as a platform for users to both request and volunteer for group walks. You will begin by setting up an account in Wingman using Google Authentication. From there, you can traverse between your personal profile page which displays your user data, as well as, the walks that you've both requested and volunteered for. Feel free to navigate around the site to both request and volunteer for additional walks as your schedule allows. Thanks for checking out Wingman and safe travels!
